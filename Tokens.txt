Tokens are reserved expressions or symbols that have a pre-defined meaning in Java, and you cannot overried this meaning.

Theses tokens are divided into 5 categories;
1- Keywords; E.g: int, boolean, switch, etc
2- Identifiers; Any variable names you declare in your program
3- Constants; final variables
4- Special Symbols; E.g: [], (), {}, ;, *, =
5- Operators; E.g arithmetic, comparison, logical, bitwise, etc.
